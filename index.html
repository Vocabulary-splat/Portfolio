      > This site

      This website was a small passion project. I made this with HTML and CSS. Thing is, however, HTML is not hard. Most programming is a spesfic subset of math under unntiuitive constrants known as computer architecture, then Operating system, then engine, and then you own work elsewhere. HTML, on the other hand is basiclly highlighting. And while I was re-making my portforlio, debating tranferring it to any standard or boring Wix or such service, I wondered how'd that reflect on me as a dev unwilling to go the extra mile. So instead I'm going the extra mile, and instead of cheaping out, I'm actually using the highlighters.
     That being said, some don't go the extra mile due to time and budget constraints and those were considered. I do not like gradients or clutter, yet, I wanted something with a professional level effort.
